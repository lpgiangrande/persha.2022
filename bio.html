PERSHA

| Bio

Director and editor for TV in her previous career, Vanessa Giangrande moves to music in 2018. Producer, composer and singer, she gives birth the same year to her 90's inspired electro-pop project, Persha. Her first EP is released in April 2018 from which two tracks are used for movie trailers on french TV channel Canal +. In November 2019, she releases her second EP, including Garbage's Stupid Girl cover and in 2021 her song Release the pressure is featured in Netflix' film Sentinelle with Olga Kurylenko. In 2022, Persha comes back with her new single Hooked (March 17th) followed closely by an explosive featuring with major synthwave artist Carpenter Brut, on his new album coming out April 1st. Two other singles will follow before the release of an 'electro-mystical' EP by the end of the year.
